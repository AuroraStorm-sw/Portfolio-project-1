Did you know?

Humans have crafted since the beginning of our existance, and while today we might not need to rely as heavily on handicraft as then, we still find joy and satisfaction in creating something with our hands.

Crafting back then was combined with storytelling - throughout the years we've shared old legends and tales while passing our knowledge.

Now, crocheting isn't that old and only dates back to the early 1700's France. Compare that to knitting, which dates back to 11th century CE Egypt, crocheting is quite the modern craft.

Still, the crocheting community is large across the world, and here at Coffee & Crochet we invite you to join us in bringing back the old tradition of crafting and storytelling - with the addition of a steaming cup of coffee.
