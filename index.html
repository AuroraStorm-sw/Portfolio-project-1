Welcome to a space dedicated to sharing, learning, and connecting, where all skill levels are welcome to weekly meetups.

Here, we want to bring together everyone who shares the passion of crafting so we can help each other grow and inspire one and another to pick that temperature blanket back up or weave in the ends of our scarves — maybe even find the courage to tackle a bedspread or an intricate piece of clothing.

This is also the perfect space for beginners who don't know how to start their journey, where you can be personally guided through the vast sea of hook sizes, patterns, and yarn weights.

And did you know?

Humans have crafted since the beginning of our existence, and while today we might not need to rely as heavily on handicraft as then, we still find joy and satisfaction in creating something with our hands.

Crafting back then was combined with storytelling - throughout the years we've shared old legends and tales while passing on our knowledge.

Now, crocheting isn't that old and only dates back to the early 1700s France. Compared to knitting, which dates back to 11th century CE Egypt, crocheting is quite the modern craft.

Still, the crocheting community is large across the world, and here at Coffee & Crochet, we invite you to join us in bringing back the old tradition of crafting and storytelling - with the addition of a steaming cup of coffee.
